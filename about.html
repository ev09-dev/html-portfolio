About Me

I thrive at the intersection of clean UX and maintainable frontend architecture. For me, great code is like a well-told story: clear, structured, and a joy to experience.

Currently diving deep into React, Express and exploring edge-side rendering. Outside the editor, I'm probably brewing pour-over coffee, contributing to open‑source, or hiking with my dog Luna.
